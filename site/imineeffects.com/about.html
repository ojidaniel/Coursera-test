Our Story

IMINE EFFECT ENTERPRISES is a privately held Corporation organized in Rivers State owned in total by its founder Ifeanyi Ukagwu. Imine Effects Articles of Incorporation were filed in 14th March, 2017. The General nature of Business is: Boutique Services, Jewelry and Accessories, Sale and Manufacturing Cosmetics.

Imine Effects Enterprises is a manufacturer of Couture and retailer of an upscale clothing line targeted at males between the ages of 20 and 60. The company creates chic lines of clothing for men that love what they choose to wear and care how they present their image in public.

Alongside the chic lines comes with Perfumes, Jewelries and accessories that is made exclusively for the fashion forward, matured and bold - those who are not afraid to stand out and break outside of the norm.

The key message associated with the Imine Effects Enterprises line is classy, upscale, versatile, and high-quality.

Our Vision

Imine Effects Enterprises intend to develop an inventory of unique fashion products that can add value of every man's wardrobe. These products, which will be sourced and manufactured in Nigeria, can be added as staple, fashionable simple pieces to enhance the image experience to men that hold fashion, quality and high-end design of utmost importance. In the future, the company hopes to develop lines of clothing for men and women.

Our Mission

It is Imine Effects Enterprises mission to bring back the enchantment of clothing and other fashionable products, all over again in the Company's couture lines, Industrializing the Art of creativity in Perfumery and other Fashion Accessories in Nigeria.
